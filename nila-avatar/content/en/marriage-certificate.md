# Marriage Registration — Sri Lanka

## How to apply
Register your marriage at the Registrar General's Department or at the Divisional Secretariat where the marriage took place.

## Required documents
- NIC copies of both parties
- Birth certificates of both parties
- Two witnesses with valid NIC
- Marriage notice (if applicable under civil law)

## Fees
Registration fee: approximately Rs. 500–1,000. Certified copy: Rs. 100–200.

## Processing time
Certificate usually issued within 1–3 working days after registration.

## Office hours
Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 4:15pm.

RESOURCES:
- Office: Registrar General's Department / Divisional Secretariat
