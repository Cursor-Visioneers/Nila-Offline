## How to apply for agricultural subsidies (step by step)

1. **Register or update your Farmer Profile** at your nearest **Agrarian Service Center** (bring NIC and land/cultivation details), or via **Agrarian.lk** when the portal is open for applications.
2. **Check which programme is active** (e.g. fertilizer subsidy, “Ketata Aruna”, or cash/voucher schemes) on agrariandept.gov.lk — programmes and forms change by season.
3. **Download the correct application form** (Sinhala/Tamil/English) from the Department website under Subsidies, or collect it from your Agrarian Service Center.
4. **Submit the completed form** with NIC copy and farmer registration details to your **Agrarian Service Center** in your DS division. Some seasons allow online submission through Agrarian.lk — follow on-screen instructions only on the official site.
5. **Fertilizer collection**: if approved, collect subsidized fertilizer from the designated center on the dates announced by the Department (keep your approval SMS/reference if issued).

**Important:** Subsidies are not issued by the Divisional Secretariat for passports or NIC — they are handled only by the Department of Agrarian Development and Agrarian Service Centers.
