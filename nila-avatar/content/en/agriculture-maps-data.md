# NRMC & CROPIX — Agriculture Maps and Data — Sri Lanka

## Natural Resources Management Centre (NRMC)
Geo-spatial and environmental data hub under the Department of Agriculture.

### Key services
- GIS maps of soil variations across agro-ecological zones
- Seasonal agro-climatology alerts
- Rainfall pattern data
- Recommendations on crops suited to specific soil conditions

## CROPIX System
Sri Lanka's official data-driven agricultural monitoring repository.

### Key services
- Early-warning on nationwide vegetable overproduction or scarcity
- Dynamic crop registries for planners and commercial growers
- Market supply visibility before planting decisions

## Access
Through DOA / NRMC official channels and CROPIX portal when published online.
