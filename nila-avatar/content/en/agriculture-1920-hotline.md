# 1920 Agriculture Advisory Service — Sri Lanka

## Purpose
Live technical helpline run by the **National Agriculture Information and Communication Centre (NAICC)** under the Department of Agriculture.

## How to call
- Dial **1920** toll-free from any local mobile or fixed network in Sri Lanka
- Speak to an **agronomist** about pests, soil problems, or crop failures

## Key services
- Active pest threat advice
- Soil deficiency guidance
- Cultivation failure troubleshooting

## Related websites
- Department of Agriculture: http://www.doa.gov.lk
- NAICC information via DOA channels

## Fees
Free (toll-free shortcode).
