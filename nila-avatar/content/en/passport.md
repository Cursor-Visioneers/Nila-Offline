# Passport — Sri Lanka

## How to apply
Apply online at **https://www.immigration.gov.lk** or visit a regional **Department of Immigration and Emigration** office.

## Required documents
- Valid NIC
- Birth certificate
- Passport-size photographs (per current specification)
- Previous passport (if renewing)

## Fees
New 36-page passport: approximately Rs. 10,000–15,000 (check current schedule on the website).

## Processing time
Normal: about 10–15 working days. Express service available at higher fee when offered.

## Contact
- **Website:** https://www.immigration.gov.lk
- **Online e-passport:** https://epassport.immigration.gov.lk (when available)
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:30am–4:15pm (confirm at office)
- **GIC:** **1919**
