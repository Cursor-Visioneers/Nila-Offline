# National Identity Card (NIC) — Sri Lanka

## How to apply
Visit the **Department for Registration of Persons (DRP)** or apply through your **Grama Niladhari** for first-time applications.

## Required documents
- Birth certificate
- Two passport-size photographs
- Proof of address
- Old NIC (for replacements)

## Fees
First NIC: free. Replacement: approximately Rs. 500–1,000 depending on service type.

## Processing time
Approximately 2–4 weeks for a new card.

## Contact
- **Website:** http://www.drp.gov.lk
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:30am–4:15pm
- **GIC:** **1919**

- **Form ROP 1** — available at registration offices
