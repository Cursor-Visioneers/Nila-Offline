# Taxes — Sri Lanka

## Inland Revenue Department (IRD)
Main authority for income tax, PAYE, and many direct taxes.

### Key services
- Income tax registration and filing
- Tax Identification Number (TIN)
- e-filing via IRD online services

### Contact
- **Website:** https://www.ird.gov.lk
- **e-filing:** https://eservices.ird.gov.lk
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:30am–4:15pm
- **GIC:** **1919**

## Sri Lanka Customs
- **Website:** https://www.customs.gov.lk — import/export duties and clearance

## Department of Excise
Excise duties on alcohol, tobacco, and related goods.

## General guidance
- Keep NIC and registration documents ready
- File returns before published deadlines to avoid penalties
- Use only official government websites for payments
