# Department of Export Agriculture (DEA) — Sri Lanka

## Purpose
Dedicated to export agricultural crops (EAC) — spices and selected cash crops (not tea, rubber, or coconut).

## Crops covered
Cinnamon, pepper, cardamom, cloves, nutmeg, coffee, cocoa, and related export crops.

## Key services
- Localized farming strategies for premium spices
- Cultivation Assistance Programs
- Post-harvest processing grants
- Business counseling for spice exports

## Who should contact DEA
Export-oriented growers, spice processors, and exporters.

## Office hours
Monday to Friday, 8:30am–4:15pm.
