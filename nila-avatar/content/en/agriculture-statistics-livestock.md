# Agriculture Statistics & Livestock — Sri Lanka

## Department of Census and Statistics (Agriculture)
Official central ledger for agricultural performance data.

### Key services
- Seasonal paddy yields (Maha and Yala seasons)
- Livestock headcounts
- Cost-of-production breakdowns
- Food security indicators

## Department of Animal Production and Health (DAPH)
Regulatory framework for livestock health and development.

### Key services
- Animal breeding and poultry management guidance
- Veterinary safety standards
- Livestock transportation permits
- Disease control guidelines
- Certifications for dairy or meat production
