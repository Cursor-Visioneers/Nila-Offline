# Department of Agriculture (DOA) — Sri Lanka

## Purpose
Technical and research backbone of Sri Lankan farming.

## Key services
- Crop production guides and cultivation manuals
- Disease identification checklists and plant protection protocols
- Coordination of national research institutes (e.g. Rice Research and Development Institute)
- Higher National Diploma in Agricultural Production Technology (NVQ Level 6) — applications posted on DOA channels

## Who should contact DOA
Farmers, extension officers, students, and agribusinesses needing technical crop advice.

## Office hours
Monday to Friday, 8:30am–4:15pm (confirm with local office).
