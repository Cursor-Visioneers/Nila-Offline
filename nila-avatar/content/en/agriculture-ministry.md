# Ministry of Agriculture & Plantation — Sri Lanka

## Purpose
Central governing body for national agricultural guidelines, policy, and high-level programs.

## Key services
- National agrarian policies and regulatory frameworks
- Large-scale modernization initiatives (e.g. Climate Smart Irrigated Agriculture Project)
- Official announcements and national budgeting plans for agriculture

## Where to go
- Ministry of Agriculture website for policy documents and press releases
- Best source for official high-level announcements

## Office hours
Typically Monday to Friday, government office hours (8:30am–4:15pm).
