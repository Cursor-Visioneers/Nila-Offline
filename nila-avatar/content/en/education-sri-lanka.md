# GCE School Exams & Education — Sri Lanka

## G.C.E. Ordinary Level (O/L) — how to register

### If you are a school candidate (most students)
1. Registration is done **through your school**, not individually online.
2. Contact your **school principal or examinations coordinator** before the deadline published by the Department of Examinations.
3. The school submits candidate lists to the Department of Examinations.
4. Collect your **index number** and exam timetable from the school before the examination.

### If you are a private candidate (not attending school)
1. Visit the official online portal: **https://onlineexams.gov.lk/eic**
2. Log in using your **NIC number** when private registration is open (check announcements on doenets.lk).
3. Select subjects, exam medium (Sinhala/Tamil/English), and pay the application fee online (card) or via postal payment as instructed.
4. Download and keep your application confirmation.

**Do not confuse this with:** marriage registration, birth registration, or passport applications — those use different offices.

## G.C.E. Advanced Level (A/L)
- School candidates: register through school (same as O/L process).
- Private candidates: https://onlineexams.gov.lk/eic when registration window is open.
- After results: university placement via University Grants Commission (UGC).

## Exam results & certificates
- Results: published on https://www.doenets.lk
- Certified copies: apply through Department of Examinations procedures announced after results.

## School enrollment (new students)
- Contact your **local school** or **Provincial Department of Education** for placement.
- Ministry of Education policy site: http://www.moe.gov.lk
- Documents often required: birth certificate, previous school records, NIC/passport of parent/guardian.

## University admission (after A/L)
- University Grants Commission (UGC): http://www.ugc.ac.lk
- Admission uses **Z-score** and official UGC handbook for the academic year.
