# Birth Certificate — Sri Lanka

## How to apply
Visit your nearest **Divisional Secretariat (DS)** office with the required documents. Registration should be done within three months of birth.

## Required documents
- Hospital birth notification letter
- Parents' NIC copies
- Marriage certificate of parents (if applicable)

## Fees
Free of charge for the first copy. Rs. 100 for subsequent copies.

## Processing time
Usually issued on the same day if documents are complete.

## Contact & hours
- **Where:** Divisional Secretariat in your area (find via your District Secretariat)
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:30am–4:15pm
- **GIC help:** **1919** (Government Information Centre)

## Forms
- **Form BDR-1** — available at the DS office
