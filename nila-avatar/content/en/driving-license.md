# Driving Licence — Sri Lanka

## How to apply
Train at an **approved driving school**, pass medical and written/practical tests, then apply at the **Department of Motor Traffic (DMT)**.

## Required documents
- Valid NIC
- Medical certificate
- Training certificate from approved driving school
- DMT application form

## Fees
Learner permit: approximately Rs. 1,500. Full licence: approximately Rs. 2,500–5,000 (varies by vehicle class).

## Processing time
Learner permit: often same day. Full licence: about 1–2 weeks after passing the practical test.

## Contact
- **Website:** http://www.motortraffic.gov.lk
- **Main office:** Department of Motor Traffic, Werahera / Colombo (confirm branch for your district)
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:30am–3:30pm
- **GIC:** **1919**
